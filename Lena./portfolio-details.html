Toxicity Inspector

Toxicity Inspector is a web-based application that uses machine learning techniques to classify whether a comment is toxic or not. Its primary goal is to help users inspect the toxicity level of comments and provide instant feedback on the obtained results to improve the accuracy of the toxicity labels and enable the model to accurately identify and categorize toxic comments. Toxicity Inspector supports multiple languages, including Arabic and English, making it accessible to a broader audience. Toxicity Inspector can help to promote a more positive and respectful online comments environment by identifying and addressing toxic comments across different languages and cultures.
